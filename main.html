Hobo Fight! is a party king of the hill game which allows Hobos to fight each other in order to win a house in town.

The more time you spend inside the house, the closer to transform it into your home.

In this crazy game, you and your friends become Hobos and so you can do everything that comes with it...like throw things, drink unknown liquids and try a pair of worn out shoes.
